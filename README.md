# 📊 RFM Customer Segmentation Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard developed using Microsoft Power BI to analyse customer purchasing behaviour through the Recency, Frequency and Monetary (RFM) model.

The objective of this analysis is to identify valuable customer segments, understand purchasing patterns, and provide actionable recommendations that support customer retention and revenue growth.

---

## Business Objectives

- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Understand customer purchasing behaviour.
- Detect customers at risk of churn.
- Support data-driven customer retention strategies.
- Improve customer lifetime value.

---

## Tools Used

- SQL
- Microsoft Power BI
- DAX
- Data Modelling
- RFM Analysis

---

## Dashboard Pages

- Executive Overview
- Customer Segmentation Analysis
- Customer Behaviour Analysis
- Business Recommendations

---

## Key Insights

- Lost Customers represent the largest customer segment, highlighting opportunities for customer re-engagement.
- Loyal Customers continue to generate significant long-term value.
- Potential Loyalists represent strong opportunities for future revenue growth.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Implement customer win-back campaigns.
- Strengthen customer loyalty programmes.
- Develop personalised marketing strategies.
- Focus on improving customer retention and customer lifetime value.
